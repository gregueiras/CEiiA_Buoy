Who We Are
I present to you an extraordinary buoy. A buoy capable of great achievements and discoveries. I present to you the Floaty McBuoy. This buoy appears in response to a knowledge and ecologic world call. Did you know more then 95% of the ocean remains unexplored by us due to extreme conditions? This represents a great potential in the public system, scientific community and offshore industries. At the same time, the ocean is more and more a polluted place thus is necessary an ecological and critical eye towards its conservation and sustainability - 14th Goal for Sustainable Development - Life Bellow Water. With this in mind, we developed a true innovative concept of ocean observation, using biodegradable and inexpensive materials to create ocean platforms for a more comprehensive vision of our vast sea. Portugal is reborning from the Discoveries Era
